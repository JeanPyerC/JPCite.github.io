This is
My Story

My name is Jean Pierre, and I recently started coding.

In my first career, I landed a career at Wayfair as their Inventory Administrator, and surprisingly, I was good at using Excel. My boss, Joe Daley, saw this in me and showed me everything he knew about Excel. I was a fast learner, and after some time, he entrusted me to run specific reports. This drove me to a new passion, to expand my knowledge. Sadly, I gave my two weeks’ notice and landed a new career creating graphs using Excel/Power Bi.

After Wayfair, I landed at Akela, and one of my main tasks was to create reports to better view the company in terms of inventory, budgets, cost, etc. However, I left the company for personal reasons and landed at Merola Tile.

At Merola Tile, I was their Jr. Purchasing Analyst. I learned a new language called SQL. Jacob Luckhart taught me everything I needed to learn in terms of SQL, and we created many reports. Being here for so long got me thinking about attending school to expand my knowledge. While there, I attended Rutgers University's continuing education program in Data Science and graduated. I learned to use Python, Tableau, and much more, so I started my side hustle in helping small companies extract information.

Here I am. Every day, I am learning something new, hoping I can expand my knowledge. Is there one thing I learned in coding, is that you will always learn something new, as there is more than one way to do something. Also, it’s normal to forget something, as long as you know how to google it.
